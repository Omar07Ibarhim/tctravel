    ​Bura evvelki proqramda Baki Samaxi hissesini qoymaq olar obede qeder olan hisseni.13:00 Lunch Break Visit to one of the most otentic, historical and naturally beauriful village of Azerbaijan, Lahic village. Historically the village dates back to IV Century but after the earthquake the village was complitely demolished. The next generation came to this village after the XVII Century from the territory of former Persian Empire. Today the village is famous for its unique landscape and also for its copper mastery. The masters are still doing handmade copper staff with old techniques. They have kept the old feature of the village despite almost three hundred years passed after their migration to this village. Together with copper mastery also another types of handmade masteries are famous here ironsmith, saddlery, wood mastery etc. The Copper Mastery Art of Azerbaijan has been included to the list of UNESCO as an Intengible Cultural Heritage of Azerbaijan.Visit to Gabala and overnight in Gabala.
