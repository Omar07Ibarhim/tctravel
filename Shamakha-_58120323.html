​Shamakha Tour

​​Sheikh Diri Bab Mausoleum
  • –
    ​The first stop of the tour is the Sheikh Diri Bab Mausoleum, located in the city of Gobustan, near the city of Shamakhi. This building is built into the rock where the cave is located, in which the followers of Sufism lived and prayed. One of these mystics, whose name was Diri Baba, was buried there. Listening to your professional guide will give you an unforgettable experience about this place.
​City of Shamakhi
  • –
    ​The second stop of this excursion will be the city of Shamakhi, which was the capital of the Shirvanshah state, which existed for almost 7 centuries. Here you will visit the Juma mosque, built in the 8th century as the first institution of the Arab caliphate of the Abbas dynasty to spread Islam in the Caucasus. This architectural monument will amaze its visitors with both its facade and interior, and the ruins of an ancient madrasah (the first Islamic school in the region)
​Lake Nohur
  • –
    ​The next point will be Lake Nohur, which will appeal to nature lovers. This lake reflects the mountains of the Greater Caucasus in its green-blue waters. Also on the territory of the lake you can visit shops with dried fruits, pastille, honey, oriental spices and Lankaran tea.
​ Tufandag ski resort
  • –
    ​You will go to the Tufandag ski resort and use the cable car to climb peaks more than 2 thousand meters high, from where you will have a view of the Bazardyuzyu - the highest peak of the Greater Caucasus in the Republic of Azerbaijan.After completing the program of the first day of the tour, our professional team will take you to the ancient city of Sheki Azerbaijan, where you will stay for one night at the five-star Sheki Palace Hotel with breakfast, located in the historical part of the city. At this hotel you will find services such as fitness and spa centers, as well as a restaurant overlooking the Sheki caravanserai
